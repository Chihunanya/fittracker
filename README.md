## Features

- **User Authentication**: Sign up and log in securely using SQLite.
- **Workout Logging**: Log Cardio, Strength, Yoga, or other workouts with duration.
- **Points System**: Earn points based on workout duration (1 point per 10 minutes).
- **Badges & Achievements**: Unlock badges for first workout, 7-day streaks, and more.
- **Weekly Goal Tracker**: Set personal weekly workout goals and track progress with a dynamic progress bar.
- **Animations & Fun Icons**: Visual emojis for workouts and celebratory confetti/rain for achievements.
- **Leaderboard**: See the top performers in your university and your rank among peers.
